Cooling Degree Day (CDD)

CDD: The number of degrees that daily average temperature is above 18 °C(65° Fahrenheit).Cooling degree-days for a given day are the number of degrees Celsius that the mean temperature is above 18 °C. If the temperature is equal to or less than 18 °C, then the number will be zero.
